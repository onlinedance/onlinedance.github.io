Classes

Join my online live classes over Zoom at a fraction of the cost you'd otherwise pay in your own country. I am a professionally trained dancer, and I can teach you pretty much any dance style at your request. Currently, group classes are available for Qafqaz and Azerbaijani National Dances (Xalq rəqsləri). Customized classes are possible at request; whether it's hip-hop or any other style, you name it. My group classes are very small in size - max 4 people per Zoom session. This allows me to keep an eye on how well you learn and correct your moves if necessary.
